**Status:** Published &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Category:** Research &nbsp;·&nbsp; 2025

Co-authored a peer-reviewed paper in the Journal of Applied Physics on measuring field-like spin-orbit torque. The work introduced an optimized Wheatstone-bridge geometry that improves signal sensitivity, enabling reliable SOT-MRAM characterization, in-situ calibration, and materials screening.

## Highlights

- Optimized Wheatstone-bridge geometry improving sensitivity for field-like spin-orbit torque
- Enables in-situ calibration, reducing experimental complexity while improving accuracy
- Supports reliable SOT-MRAM characterization, process control, and materials screening
