A Unity puzzle adventure built around lightning-pathway mechanics and space-themed environments, where players solve electrical and spatial reasoning challenges to progress. Currently an early-stage prototype with an expandable framework for multi-level progression.

## Highlights

- Lightning-pathway core mechanics centered on spatial reasoning
- Space-themed environments with custom art and models
- Expandable prototype framework designed for multi-level progression
- In active early development

## Tech stack

C# · Unity · ShaderLab
