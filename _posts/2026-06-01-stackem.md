A fast-paced competitive game where two players race to build the tallest tower while dodging obstacles and sabotaging each other. Built in Unity with custom ShaderLab and HLSL graphics and deployed to WebGL for browser-based, cross-platform play.

## Highlights

- Competitive multiplayer tower-building with player sabotage mechanics
- Real-time graphics using custom ShaderLab and HLSL shaders
- WebGL deployment for browser-based cross-platform play
- Dynamic terrain obstacles and physics-based building interactions
