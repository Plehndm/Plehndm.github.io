Hi, I'm **David Plehn** — a second-year at **UC Irvine** double-majoring in **Computer Science (Intelligent Systems)** and **Quantitative Economics**, with a minor in Statistics. I build things across the stack: large language models from first principles, multi-agent AI platforms, and cross-platform apps shipped to web, iOS, and Android.

I'm currently a **Systems Engineer Intern at [Lockheed Martin](https://www.lockheedmartin.com/)** (Python automation of mission-critical systems) and an **AI Trainer at [Handshake AI](https://joinhandshake.com/)** (benchmarking and evaluating LLMs across text, audio, and vision). Previously I was a **Research Assistant at the University of Denver**, where I co-authored a peer-reviewed paper in the *[Journal of Applied Physics](https://doi.org/10.1063/5.0300521)* on spin-orbit-torque measurement.

I'm looking for **software engineering, data science, and AI internships**. Have a look at my [projects](/projects/) and [publications](/publications/), grab my [résumé](/assets/pdf/David_Plehn_Resume.pdf), or reach out at [dplehn@uci.edu](mailto:dplehn@uci.edu).
