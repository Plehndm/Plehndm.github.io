**Status:** In development · **Role:** Solo developer · **Engine:** Unity

A local two-player competitive game where players race to build the tallest tower fastest — navigating terrain obstacles and opponent sabotage along the way.

## Tech stack

- **Engine:** Unity
- **Graphics:** custom shaders (ShaderLab / HLSL)

## Highlights

- Heavy **shader and graphics work** for visual polish.
- Head-to-head competitive multiplayer mechanics.
- Terrain and obstacle systems.
