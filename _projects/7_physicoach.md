**Status:** In development · **Role:** Solo developer · **Platforms:** iOS · Android

A mobile coaching app that helps users follow exercise and rehab routines, track progress over time, and get guided instructions with motivational feedback.

## Tech stack

- **Framework:** Flutter / Dart, built with FlutterFlow
- **Backend:** Firebase

## Highlights

- Routine planning with progress tracking over time.
- Motivational performance-feedback loop.
- Native iOS/Android delivery from a single Flutter codebase.
